Area Accomodations

Speculator and Lake Pleasant are towns in the Adirondack park. This means there aren't any chain hotels nearby — or any place big enough for everybody. So we've gathered a list of the area lodging for use on Friday night (or longer if you need it). Most of these are small places with just a few rooms available, and because it's the Adirondacks in the summer season, these places will probably fill up fast, so book soon!
