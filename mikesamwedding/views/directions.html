Travelling in

Speculator is located on Lake Pleasant in the Adirondack Park. It is where Routes 8 & 30 intersect. For reference, it's about an hour west of Lake George, 45 minutes south of Blue Mountain Lake, and a hour north of Gloversville.

For those coming in from far away, the closest airport is located in Albany, NY (ALB). From Albany, it'll take around 1.5 hours to drive to up to Speculator. Additionally, the Syracuse airport (SYR) and Plattsburgh airport (PBG) are both about 2 hours away.
